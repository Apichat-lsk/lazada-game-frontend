Terms & Conditions (T&C) — 9.9 Mega Brands Sale Microsite Below is a draft of the Terms & Conditions for participating in the Lazada 9.9 Mega Brands Sale Microsite game, based on the project brief: 1. Eligibility & Participation The activity is open to all users accessing the Lazada app during the campaign period. Each user can participate only once per day. The game resets daily at 20:00. To participate, users must log in or register with accurate information, including: Full name Delivery address Email address Mobile phone number Username Once registration is completed, information cannot be edited. 2. Consent By joining, users agree to all Terms & Conditions and Lazada’s Privacy Policy. 3. Gameplay After registration and T&C acceptance, users may read the instructions and then start the quiz. Each round consists of 5 multiple-choice questions about shopping. Each question must be answered within 10 seconds. 4. Passing Criteria Users who answer at least 4 out of 5 questions correctly will be awarded a downloadable certificate. Users who do not pass may try again on the next day after 20:00. 5. Leaderboard & Scoring Scores are based on the number of correct answers and response speed. Real-time rankings and scores are shown on the leaderboard following each attempt. The leaderboard resets every day at 20:00. 6. Rewards & Announcements Daily winners are announced via the app at 20:00 the day after participation. Winners will also be notified by email. Prizes will be delivered to the address provided upon registration. All rewards are subject to availability, and Lazada reserves the right to change prize details with equivalent value. 7. Fraud Detection & Disqualification Lazada reserves the right to disqualify users if evidence of fraud is found, such as: Unusually high, inhuman scores Use of bots or automated tools Multiple account registrations with the same IP address Providing false information Use of Lazada or Alibaba employee emails Disqualified users will be barred from further participation and ineligible for prizes. 8. Final Decision All Lazada decisions regarding winner selection, fraud detection, and rewards are final. Note: These T&C are tailored directly from the provided campaign brief. All data collected is exclusively for user verification and prize fulfillment.
